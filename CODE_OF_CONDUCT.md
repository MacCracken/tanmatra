# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
